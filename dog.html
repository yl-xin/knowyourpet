BENEFITS/OF/DOG

GET SICK LESS

People with dogs seem to get ill less frequently and less severely than people - especially children - with cats or no pets.

MORE EXERCISE

Dogs have the added benefit of needing to be walked and played with numerous times a day. This means that many dog owners are getting 30 minutes of exercise a day.

HEART HEALTH

Just the act of petting a dog lowers heart rate and blood pressure. some studies show pet owners have slightly lower cholesterol and are more likely to survive a heart attack.
